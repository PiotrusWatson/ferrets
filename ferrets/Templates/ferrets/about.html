About

Ferreted Away is a site made for student, by students. It's like StudentVoice meets GumTree, but better. On Ferreted Away you can buy and sell anything, and it's completely free to join.

First made in 2016 by a group of stressed and exhausted students, Ferreted Away fought and clawed its way into existence over the course of several horrific months. {% endblock %}
